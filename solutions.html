Solutions



Feeling is the first step. Now, let's heal! Everyone heals in different ways. Below, there are several ways to heal. Remember, this process will take time and patience. Enjoy the journey!



Psychotherapy

Psychotherapy is where a client talks to a therapists about their problems. It helps get issues off their chest, and helps improve emotions and behaviors. It helps someone to start understanding themselves and leads to a brighter journey. In a study, 75% of people who started psychotherapy had benefits from it (American Psychiatric Association, 2019).

Take Care of Yourself

Eating healthy is one of the best ways to maintain a healthy and well-functioning mindset. Eating nutritious foods can help improve focus and concentration, helping us make better decisions and feel uplifted. Exercising also helps relieve anxiety and depression symptoms, making you feel better inside and out.

Watch this TED-ED video for a more scientific explanation. To learn more about how food affects mental health, click here!

Speak to a Trusted Adult or Friend


You are not alone, even if it may feel like it. Talk to your parents, counselor, or friends to get help and support. Tell them how you are feeling, and they may be able to provide you a solution. If not, try talking to a professional expert.
