Our Story

Feeling and Healing is a company that is dedicated to guide you to achieve nirvana. Due to the Covid pandemic, there has been an increase in depression and other psychological problems. Isolation and the virtual life has impacted many in negative ways. Our mission is to aid everyone who is dealing with mental problems. We help you feel your emotions and express them in a calm manner. Then, we provide guidance to heal you from your problems. Our mental health conference is set every year. The purpose of our mental health conference is to educate people about psychological problems and create a supportive environment for growth.

Threats of Mental Health

Mental Health doesn't discriminate. In fact, about 26% of Americans who are 18 or older in age have some sort of mental disorder (John Hopkins 2022). Mental health not only causes major depressive moods, but can also cause serious consequences, such as suicides. For example, many people who committed suicide are victims of some type of mental disorder, which can be diagnosable (John Hopkins 2022).
